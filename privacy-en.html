Article 1 (General Provisions)

segawa takeshi (hereinafter "the Provider") establishes this Privacy Policy (hereinafter "this Policy") regarding the handling of personal information of users of the iOS application "Yametter" (hereinafter "the App").

The Provider may collect the following information when you use the App:

  1. Account information obtained through Sign in with Apple (user identifier, email address, etc.)

  3. Log information related to App usage (access date/time, features used, etc.)

Collected information is used for the following purposes:

  1. Providing, operating, and improving the App
  2. Responding to user support inquiries

  5. Analyzing usage and developing new features

Article 4 (Sharing Information with Third Parties)

The Provider will not provide users' personal information to third parties except in the following cases:

  1. When the user has given consent
  2. When required by law

  4. When particularly necessary for improving public health or promoting the sound development of children
  5. When necessary to cooperate with a government agency or local government in carrying out duties prescribed by law

The App displays advertisements using Google AdMob, provided by Google LLC. Google AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as advertising IDs) and other information to optimize ad delivery. For details on the information collected and how it is used, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Users can reset their advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads through their device settings.

Article 6 (Analytics and Crash Reporting — Firebase)

The App uses Firebase, provided by Google LLC, for app analytics and crash reporting. Firebase may collect device information, usage logs, and other data for these purposes. For details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Article 7 (Apple Services)

The App uses "Sign in with Apple" provided by Apple Inc. For details on how Apple handles your information, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.

Article 8 (Data Security)

The Provider takes appropriate security measures to prevent leakage, loss, or alteration of collected personal information. However, complete security over the internet cannot be guaranteed.

Article 9 (Access, Correction, and Deletion)

Users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal information held by the Provider. Please contact us at the address listed at the end of this Policy.

Please note that deleting your account will result in the deletion of all data stored within the App.

Article 10 (Children's Privacy)

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete such information.
